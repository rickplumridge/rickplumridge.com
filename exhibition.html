Transform your foyer
into a gallery space

A temporary exhibition of beautiful and inspirational photographic works will transform your building foyer into a place of interest and warmth.  The exhibition benefits your building by inviting its occupants and visitors to enjoy the change in ambience.

There is no cost to you - we set up and dismantle all exhibition components.  All stands are supplied and installed with lighting having been tagged and tested on a regular basis.

The exhibition stands are modular (2m x 2m) and can be set up to fit your foyer size and floor plan easily.  Photographic prints range in size from 1m to 2m wide. The suggested exhibition duration is 2-4wks.

Insurance

We provide all liability insurance at each exhibition location covering installation and removal, slips/trips, object falling, obstruction/fire and theft/vandalism.

Rick Plumridge has chosen Bellini frames for his images as they are the best available frames and they complement the high quality and detail of the images, creating a true piece of artwork. Bellini’s extensive collection of Italian-made frames are finished by hand, using century-old techniques. The aesthetic potential of these fine mouldings has an immediate and compelling appeal to the most discerning eye.
